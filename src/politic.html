Privacy Policy

Chaincomply Ltd (hereinafter referred to as “we”, “our”, “us” or the “Company”), operating the website https://www.chaincomply.net, is a Cypriot company registered under number HE 480964, with its registered address at Thivon 5, Kaimakli, 1026 Nicosia, Cyprus

1.Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Chaincomply Ltd protects and processes personal data of individuals who access or use our website and services (hereinafter referred to as the “Services”).

We are committed to processing your personal data transparently, responsibly, and in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (Regulation (EU) 2016/679) (“GDPR”) and applicable Cyprus data protection laws.

Effective as of October 21 2025. The type of personal data we collect and process depends on the nature of your interaction with us and the Services requested.

2. What We Collect

Our Personal Data Protection Policy governs how we use and store your data. Chaincomply Ltd acts as the Data Controller of any personal data you provide.

We collect only the minimal data necessary to operate and improve our Services. This may include:
• Name, surname
• Email address
• Telephone number
• Company or organization name (if provided)


Your personal information is collected when you fill in forms on our website, such as the contact form, or when you communicate with us directly via email.

Your data may also be processed by authorized employees, contractors, or partners who assist in delivering our Services, strictly under confidentiality and data protection obligations.

3. Why We Collect Your Data

We collect and process personal data for the following purposes:
• To respond to your inquiries or requests
• To provide information about our Services
• To maintain communication and business relationships
• To comply with legal or regulatory obligations

Legal Basis:
• Your consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR)
• Performance of a contract or steps prior to entering a contract (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR)
• Compliance with legal obligations (Article 6(1)(c) GDPR)
• Our legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR), such as maintaining secure operations and improving user experience

4. Who Receives Your Personal Data

Your personal data may be accessed by:
• Authorized employees or contractors of Chaincomply Ltd;
• Service providers and partners who assist with website hosting, email services, or data management;
• Legal or regulatory authorities, when required by law.

All parties that process personal data on our behalf are contractually obligated to comply with applicable data protection standards and confidentiality requirements.

5. Transfer of Data Outside the EEA

Your personal data may be transferred outside the European Economic Area (EEA) only when necessary and always in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

In such cases, we ensure that adequate safeguards are implemented, including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) as approved under GDPR Article 46.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law.

Once no longer needed, the data is securely deleted or anonymized. In certain cases (e.g., for archiving, research, or legal claims), data may be retained for a longer period, provided that proper safeguards are applied.

7. Your Data Protection Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:
• Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
• Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
• Erasure (“Right to be Forgotten”): Request deletion of your data when there is no lawful reason to retain it.
• Restriction: Request limitation of processing under certain circumstances.
• Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interest.
• Data Portability: Request transfer of your data to another controller, where applicable.
• Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw previously granted consent at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.
